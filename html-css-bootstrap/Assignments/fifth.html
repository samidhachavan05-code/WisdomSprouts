My Favourite Hobby

My favourite hobbies include Making rangoli, Watching movies, and Cooking.

Why I Like it

My favourite hobbies are making rangoli, watching movies, and cooking because they help me relax, express my creativity, and stay motivated. I like making rangoli because it allows me to express my artistic skills using different colors and patterns, and it gives me inner satisfaction. Watching movies helps me reduce stress and refresh my mind while also improving my imagination and understanding of emotions. Cooking is another hobby I enjoy because it gives me happiness and confidence, and I like experimenting with new recipes. These hobbies help me maintain a healthy balance between my studies and personal life and keep me positive and energetic.

How I Spend Time Doing It

I spend my free time doing my favourite hobbies such as making rangoli, watching movies, and cooking. I usually make rangoli during festivals, weekends, or special occasions, where I enjoy creating new designs and using different color combinations. I watch movies in my free time or during breaks to relax and refresh my mind. I also spend time cooking by trying new recipes and helping my family in the kitchen, especially on holidays. Spending time on these activities helps me feel happy, relaxed, and refreshed, and it improves my creativity and time management skills.

My Routine

I start my day early in the morning and begin with basic activities like getting ready and having breakfast. After that, I focus on my studies and college work, attending classes and completing assignments. I also spend time learning new technical skills and improving my knowledge related to my field. During the day, I help my family whenever needed. In the evening, I relax by watching movies or engaging in my hobbies like making rangoli or cooking. At night, I revise what I have learned, plan for the next day, and then go to sleep. This routine helps me stay organized, productive, and balanced.
