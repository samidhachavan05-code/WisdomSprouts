Flower's World

Flowers Make Our Life Beautiful!

Flowers are the vibrant, reproductive parts of plants that bring beauty to nature and joy to our surroundings. They attract bees and butterflies and support biodiversity. Flowers symbolize love, celebration, and peace.

Every flower is a soul blossoming in nature

Rose

Rose is a symbol of love and beauty.

Rose is one of the most popular flowers in the world. It belongs to the genus Rosa and has over 300 species.
Red – Love and romance
White – Peace and purity
Yellow – Friendship and joy
Pink – Grace and admiration

Roses are used in gardens, bouquets, perfumes, cosmetics, and medicines.

Sunflower

Sunflower is a symbol of happiness and positivity.

Sunflowers turn toward the sun. They can grow up to 3–4 meters tall. Sunflower seeds are edible and used to make oil.

Tulip

Tulip represents elegance and grace.

Tulips are spring flowers from Central Asia. They are associated with the Netherlands. Tulips bloom for a short period and are admired worldwide.

Lotus

Lotus symbolizes purity and is the national flower of India.

Lotus grows in muddy water. It opens during the day and closes at night. Flowers, seeds, and stem are all useful.
